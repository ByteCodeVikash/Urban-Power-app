## 4. Common Issues & Self-Help

### 4.1 I can't log in to my account

- Ensure your mobile number is entered correctly with the correct country code (+91 for India)
- Check that OTP messages are not blocked by your carrier or spam filter
- Wait 60 seconds and request a new OTP
- If the issue persists, email support@urbanpowers.com with your registered number

### 4.2 My booking was confirmed but no professional arrived

- Check the booking status in **My Bookings** — look for any cancellation or reschedule notification
- Contact the support team via **Help & Support** in the App
- If no resolution, email support@urbanpowers.com with your booking ID
- You are eligible for a full refund in this case (see Refund Policy)

### 4.3 I want to cancel or reschedule my booking

- Go to **My Bookings** → Select booking → **Cancel Booking** or **Reschedule**
- Free cancellation is available up to 4 hours before service time
- See [`RefundPolicy.md`](./RefundPolicy.md) for full details

### 4.4 I was charged incorrectly

- Go to **My Bookings** → Select booking → **Report an Issue** → **Payment Issue**
- Provide the correct amount you expected and any supporting evidence
- Our billing team will review and process any applicable refund within 5–7 business days

### 4.5 How do I delete my account?

- Go to **Account → Settings → Delete My Account**
- Or email privacy@urbanpowers.com with subject "Account Deletion Request"
- See [`DeleteAccountPolicy.md`](./DeleteAccountPolicy.md) for full details

### 4.6 The app is crashing or not loading

- Ensure you have a stable internet connection
- Force close the app and reopen
- Check if an update is available on Google Play
- Clear app cache: Android Settings → Apps → Urban Power → Clear Cache
- If the issue persists, email support@urbanpowers.com with your device model and Android version

### 4.7 I have a complaint about a service professional

- Report immediately via **My Bookings → Report an Issue → Professional Conduct**
- All conduct complaints are investigated within 48 hours
- We take safety and professional conduct extremely seriously

**Step 3:** Our Grievance Officer will respond within **15 days** as required under the IT Act, 2000
