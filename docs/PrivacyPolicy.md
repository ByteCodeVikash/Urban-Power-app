## Introduction

Urban Power ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use the Urban Power mobile application ("App") and the services offered through it. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please discontinue use of the App immediately.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Identification Information

When you register for an account, we collect:

- Full name
- Mobile phone number
- Email address
- Profile photo (optional)

### 1.2 Location Information

With your explicit consent, we collect your device's precise geographic location to:

- Show nearby available service professionals
- Auto-populate your service address
- Estimate arrival and service times

Location access is requested only when the App is in use. You may revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings.

### 1.3 Payment Information

Payments are processed exclusively through Razorpay, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. Urban Power does not store, process, or have access to your full card numbers, CVV, or net banking credentials on our servers.

### 1.4 Booking and Transaction Data

We retain records of your bookings, service history, invoices, and payment receipts to provide account management features and resolve disputes.

### 1.5 Usage and Analytics Data

We collect information about how you interact with the App, including:

- Screens visited and features used
- Search queries and service selections
- Session duration and in-app events

This data is collected in anonymized or aggregated form via Firebase Analytics.

### 1.6 Device Information

We collect:

- Device model and operating system version
- Device identifiers (for push notifications)
- App version and crash reports

### 1.7 Communications

If you contact our support team, we retain records of that correspondence to resolve your issue and improve our service quality.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

| Purpose                                         | Data Used                                   |
| ----------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| Account creation and authentication             | Name, phone, email                          |
| Processing bookings and assigning professionals | Name, phone, address, booking details       |
| Payment processing                              | Transaction amount, order ID (via Razorpay) |
| Sending transactional notifications             | Phone (SMS), email, push token              |
| Personalizing service recommendations           | Booking history, location                   |

We do not use your data for unsolicited marketing without your explicit opt-in consent.

## 3. Sharing of Your Information

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

**3.1 Service Professionals**  
We share your name, contact number, and service address with the assigned technician or professional solely to fulfill your booking. This information is not retained by the professional beyond the service date.

**3.2 Payment Processors**  
Transaction details are shared with Razorpay solely to process your payment. Razorpay's privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy.

**3.3 Analytics Providers**  
We use Firebase Analytics (Google) to understand app usage. Only anonymized, non-personally identifiable data is shared. Google's data practices are governed by their privacy policy.

**3.4 Legal and Regulatory Obligations**  
We may disclose your information when required by applicable law, court order, regulatory authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Urban Power, our users, or the public.

**3.5 Business Transfers**  
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change via in-app notification or email.

**We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for commercial or marketing purposes.**

---

## 4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active, or as required to provide services and comply with legal obligations. Specifically:

- **Account data:** Retained for the duration of your account, plus 2 years after deletion for legal compliance
- **Booking and transaction records:** Retained for 7 years as required under Indian financial record-keeping laws
- **Analytics data:** Retained in anonymized form for up to 26 months

You may request deletion of your account and personal data at any time. See Section 7 for instructions.

## 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, including:

- **HTTPS/TLS encryption** for all data transmitted between the App and our servers
- **Encrypted storage** for sensitive data at rest
- **Access controls** limiting data access to authorized personnel only
- **Regular security assessments** of our infrastructure

While we take every reasonable precaution, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security against all threats.

## 6. Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

- **Right to Access:** Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
- **Right to Correction:** Update or correct inaccurate information via your profile settings or by contacting us
- **Right to Deletion:** Request deletion of your personal data (see Section 7)
- **Right to Restrict Processing:** Request that we limit how we use your data in specific circumstances
- **Right to Withdraw Consent:** Revoke consent for location access or push notifications at any time through your device settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **privacy@urbanpowers.com**.

---

## 7. Account and Data Deletion

You have the right to delete your Urban Power account and associated personal data at any time.

**How to request deletion:**

1. Open the Urban Power App
2. Go to **Account → Settings → Delete Account**
3. Confirm your identity and submit the request

Alternatively, email **privacy@urbanpowers.com** with subject line: **"Account Deletion Request"** including your registered mobile number.

**What happens after deletion:**

- Your profile, saved addresses, and preferences are deleted immediately
- Booking history and payment records are retained for 7 years as required by applicable financial regulations
- Your data will be fully purged from active systems within **30 days** of the request

For full details, see [`DeleteAccountPolicy.md`](./DeleteAccountPolicy.md).

Each of these third-party SDKs operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.
