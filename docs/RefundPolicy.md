# Refund & Cancellation Policy

**App Name:** Urban Power  
**Package:** com.urbanpower.app  
**Entity:** Urban Power Services Pvt. Ltd.  
**Last Updated:** June 1, 2025  
**Effective Date:** June 1, 2025

---

## Introduction

Urban Power is committed to delivering high-quality home services every time. We understand that plans change, and we have designed our cancellation and refund policy to be fair, transparent, and easy to understand. Please read this policy carefully before making a booking.

---

## 1. Cancellation by Customer

### 1.1 Free Cancellation Window

You may cancel any booking **free of charge** if the cancellation is made at least **4 hours before** the scheduled service start time.

### 1.2 Late Cancellation

If a booking is cancelled within **4 hours** of the scheduled service start time, a cancellation fee of **₹99 or 20% of the booking value** (whichever is lower) will be charged. The remaining balance will be refunded to your original payment method or credited to your Urban Power Wallet.

### 1.3 No-Show

If you are unavailable at the service address at the scheduled time and no prior cancellation was made, the **full booking amount is non-refundable**. This compensates the service professional for their time and travel.

### 1.4 How to Cancel

Cancellations must be processed through the App:

1. Open Urban Power App → **My Bookings**
2. Select the booking you wish to cancel
3. Tap **"Cancel Booking"** and confirm

Cancellations via phone calls or email are **not accepted** and will not be considered valid.

---

## 2. Cancellation by Urban Power

Urban Power reserves the right to cancel a booking in the following circumstances:

- Non-availability of a qualified service professional in your area
- Safety concerns at the service location as reported by the professional
- Unforeseen circumstances such as severe weather, natural disasters, or public emergencies
- Fraudulent, suspicious, or violating bookings

**In all such cases:**

- You will receive a **full refund** of the booking amount within 5–7 business days, OR
- You may reschedule the booking at no additional charge, at your preference

Urban Power will notify you as early as possible in the event of a cancellation initiated by us.

---

## 3. Rescheduling Policy

### 3.1 Free Rescheduling Window

You may reschedule a booking at no charge if the reschedule request is made at least **2 hours before** the scheduled service start time.

### 3.2 Late Rescheduling

Rescheduling requests made **within 2 hours** of the service start time may be treated as a late cancellation at our discretion, subject to the cancellation fee described in Section 1.2.

### 3.3 Maximum Reschedules

Each booking may be rescheduled a maximum of **2 times**. A third rescheduling request will be treated as a cancellation and the applicable cancellation fee will apply.

---

## 4. Refund Eligibility

You are entitled to a refund in the following situations:

| Situation                                        | Refund                                              |
| ------------------------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Service booked and paid for, but not delivered   | Full refund                                         |
| Service cancelled by Urban Power                 | Full refund                                         |
| Duplicate payment for the same booking           | Excess amount refunded in full                      |
| Overcharge (amount billed exceeds quoted price)  | Difference refunded after verification              |
| Service quality complaint raised within 24 hours | Partial or full refund, or re-service at discretion |
| Free cancellation window respected               | Full refund                                         |
| Late cancellation                                | Refund minus cancellation fee                       |

### 4.1 Service Quality Complaints

If you are dissatisfied with a completed service, you must:

1. Raise a complaint within **24 hours** of service completion via **My Bookings → Report an Issue**
2. Describe the issue clearly and attach relevant photos if applicable

Our quality team will review the complaint within 24–48 hours and may offer:

- A re-service at no additional charge
- A partial refund to your original payment method
- Full Urban Power Wallet credit

---

## 5. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will **not** be issued in the following cases:

- Services that were fully and satisfactorily completed
- Dissatisfaction arising from requests outside the originally booked service scope
- Complaints raised more than **24 hours** after service completion
- Promotional or discounted bookings explicitly marked as non-refundable at the time of booking
- No-show situations (Section 1.3)

**Urban Power Wallet Credits:**  
Wallet credits used toward a booking payment are non-refundable to the original payment method. Upon cancellation or refund, the equivalent value will be re-credited to your Urban Power Wallet.

---

## 6. Refund Process and Timeline

| Payment Method      | Refund Timeline          |
| ------------------- | ------------------------ |
| Urban Power Wallet  | Instant (within minutes) |
| UPI                 | 1–3 business days        |
| Debit / Credit Card | 5–7 business days        |
| Net Banking         | 5–7 business days        |

- Refunds are initiated within **2 business days** of approval
- Refund approval is sent via in-app notification and email
- Timeline after initiation depends on your bank or payment provider
- Refund status can be tracked in **My Bookings → Booking Details**

If you have not received a refund beyond the stated timeline, contact us at **support@urbanpowers.com** with your booking ID.

---

## 7. Service Warranty

Urban Power offers a **7-day service warranty** on all completed jobs. If the same issue recurs within 7 days of the original service:

1. Report via **My Bookings → Report an Issue**
2. We will dispatch a professional to resolve the issue at **no additional charge**

The warranty applies only if:

- The issue is directly related to the original service performed
- No third party has attempted repair or modification after our service
- The complaint is raised within 7 days of the original service date

---

## 8. How to Raise a Refund Request

1. Open the Urban Power App and navigate to **My Bookings**
2. Select the booking for which you are requesting a refund
3. Tap **"Report an Issue"**
4. Select the appropriate reason and provide a description
5. Attach supporting photos if applicable
6. Submit the request

**Our support team will:**

- Acknowledge your request within **4 business hours**
- Resolve and approve/reject within **24–48 hours**
- Process approved refunds within 2 business days

For escalations beyond 48 hours, email **support@urbanpowers.com** with your booking ID and registered phone number.
