# Account Deletion Policy

**App Name:** Urban Power  
**Package:** com.urbanpower.app  
**Entity:** Urban Power Services Pvt. Ltd.  
**Last Updated:** June 1, 2025  
**Effective Date:** June 1, 2025

---

## Overview

Urban Power respects your right to control your personal data. This policy explains how to request deletion of your Urban Power account, what data is deleted, what data is retained for legal compliance, and the timelines involved.

This policy is provided in compliance with **Google Play's Data Deletion Requirements** for apps that enable account creation.

---

## 1. Your Right to Delete

You have the right to request permanent deletion of your Urban Power account and the personal data associated with it at any time, without penalty. Deletion is irreversible once processed.

---

## 2. How to Delete Your Account

### Method 1 — In-App (Recommended)

1. Open the **Urban Power App**
2. Navigate to **Account → Settings**
3. Scroll to the bottom and tap **"Delete My Account"**
4. Read the confirmation screen explaining what will be deleted
5. Enter your registered mobile number or OTP to confirm identity
6. Tap **"Confirm Deletion"**

Your deletion request will be submitted immediately and processed within **30 days**.

### Method 2 — Email Request

If you cannot access the App, send a deletion request to:

**Email:** privacy@urbanpowers.com  
**Subject:** Account Deletion Request  
**Include:**

- Your registered mobile number
- Your registered email address (if any)
- A brief statement requesting account deletion

We will verify your identity and confirm receipt of your request within **3 business days**.

---

## 3. Data Deleted Upon Request

Upon successful verification and processing of a deletion request, the following data will be **permanently deleted**:

| Data Type                                    | Deleted                                   |
| -------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Account profile (name, email, profile photo) | ✅ Yes                                    |
| Registered phone number                      | ✅ Yes                                    |
| Saved addresses                              | ✅ Yes                                    |
| Urban Power Wallet balance and credits       | ✅ Yes (unused balance forfeited)         |
| Push notification tokens                     | ✅ Yes                                    |
| App preferences and settings                 | ✅ Yes                                    |
| Active and pending bookings                  | ✅ Yes (subject to active booking clause) |

---

## 4. Data Retained for Legal Compliance

Certain data must be retained even after account deletion to comply with applicable Indian laws and regulations:

| Data Type                       | Retention Period                   | Legal Basis                           |
| ------------------------------- | ---------------------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| Booking and transaction records | 7 years                            | Income Tax Act, 1961; GST regulations |
| Payment history and invoices    | 7 years                            | Financial record-keeping requirements |
| Dispute and complaint records   | 3 years                            | Consumer Protection Act, 2019         |
| Fraud investigation records     | Duration of investigation + 1 year | IT Act, 2000                          |

Retained data is stored securely, access-restricted, and used solely for legal compliance purposes. It is **not used for any marketing or operational purposes** after account deletion.

---

## 5. Active Bookings Clause

If you have an **active or upcoming booking** at the time of a deletion request:

- The deletion will be **paused** until all active bookings are completed or cancelled
- You will be notified of this via email and in-app notification
- Once all bookings are resolved, the deletion will proceed automatically

Alternatively, you may cancel all active bookings first and then re-submit your deletion request.

---

## 6. Urban Power Wallet Balance

Any unused Urban Power Wallet credits at the time of account deletion are **non-refundable** and will be forfeited upon account deletion. We recommend spending or using any remaining balance before submitting a deletion request.

---

## 7. Deletion Timeline

| Step                                      | Timeline                              |
| ----------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| Request received and identity verified    | Within 3 business days                |
| Active bookings resolved (if applicable)  | As needed                             |
| Personal data deleted from active systems | Within 30 days of request             |
| Retained legal data purged                | As per retention periods in Section 4 |
| Confirmation email sent                   | Upon completion of deletion           |

---

## 8. Re-Registration After Deletion

After your account is deleted, you may create a new Urban Power account using the same phone number. A new account will have no connection to your previous booking history, wallet balance, or preferences, as all prior data will have been permanently deleted.
